Bakery

La Panera is a Buenos Aires based bakery where bread, sweets and cakes are baked everyday!

Starting and running a bakery seems simple enough -- create delicious treats with sweet, gooey frosting and watch the money roll in.

However, no matter how yummy or sugary your baked goods, you have to open your doors and get the customers to your bakery if you want to share your pastries, bread, cupcakes, and other treats with the world. One quick and easy method to increase sales at your bakery is through creative text marketing messages.
